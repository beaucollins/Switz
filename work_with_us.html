So how much do you know about technology?

A lot. We know tech culture very, very well and know how to speak that language. As creatives we’ve worked on Microsoft, Apple, Google, HP, Sun Microsystems, T-Mobile and a host of others. As producers we’ve produced launch campaigns for Apple, HP, Microsoft, Gateway, T-Mobile, RIM, among others. Steve Jobs – you may have heard of him – has world premiered our Apple ads so often over the years, it’s hard to keep track.
